Megan McShea, archivist

My archival practice is dedicated to collaborating with clients to meet their goals for stewardship of their archives. I center collaboration and respect for the knowledge and expertise of others, and work to build capacity wherever archives are housed, through projects that value and sustain archival labor, whoever carries it out.

Expertise and experience

  • Collection assessment
  • Collection management
  • Archival arrangement and description
  • Digitization and preservation, especially for analog audiovisual materials
  • Grant writing
  • Project management
  • Standards implementation
  • Workflow development
  • Digital asset management

Current projects

  • Photo Archives Digitization project, AFRO Charities, Inc.
