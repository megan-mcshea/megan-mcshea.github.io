Megan McShea, archivist

Bio

Megan McShea (she/her) is an independent archivist based in Baltimore, MD. Current projects focus on archival processing, systems migration, digital preservation, digitization, and workflow development with clients Afro Charities, Inc., stewards of the AFRO American Newspaper Archives, the Human Studies Film Archives at the Smithsonian Institution, and the Mid-Atlantic Regional Moving Image Archive (MARMIA). She has taught workshops through the Society of American Archivists, the Association of Moving Image Archivists, and the community archives program at the University of Baltimore.
